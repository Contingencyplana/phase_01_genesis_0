# Meta Story Arcs

Meta story arcs represent moments of progress in the evolution of the Kaleidoscope world.

Unlike normal story arcs, which describe locations and events within the world, meta arcs reflect the growth of the project itself.

These scenes appear rarely and are intended to feel special.

## Types of Meta Arcs

### Progress

Dream-like scenes that hint at the Island of Progress.

These occur occasionally during development and reflect gradual advancement.

### Breakthroughs

Scenes representing important discoveries or technological breakthroughs within the Kaleidoscope world.

These appear when major capabilities are unlocked.

### Milestones

Scenes marking the completion of major stages of development.

At these moments, the Island of Progress may become a real location rather than a dream.

## Meta Arc Ranges

Meta story arcs occupy the reserved scene identifier range:

9000–9999

Initial meta-arc folders include:

9000_progress  
9100_breakthroughs  
9200_milestones  
9300_waypoints  
9400_achievements  

These arcs represent different kinds of progress in the evolution of the Kaleidoscope world.

Additional meta arcs may be defined later if needed, but the total number should remain small to preserve clarity and avoid unnecessary complexity.

## Design Principles

Meta scenes should be:

- rare
- short
- symbolic

They exist to celebrate progress and to remind players that the world itself is evolving.
