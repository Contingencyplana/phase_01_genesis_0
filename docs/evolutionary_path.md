# Kaleidoscope — Evolutionary Path

This document describes the long-term evolutionary path of the Kaleidoscope ecosystem.

Kaleidoscope begins as a simple collaborative storytelling space and gradually evolves into a game creation platform, a creative ecosystem, and eventually a safe environment for advanced artificial intelligence research.

The stages below describe this progression.

---

# Stage 1 — Interactive Fiction

The seed stage.

Kaleidoscope begins as a shared narrative environment where people contribute fragments of story.

Characteristics:

- collaborative storytelling
- text-first interaction
- simple participation
- many small contributions

Goal:

Create **narrative gravity** — a world people want to explore and contribute to.

---

# Stage 2 — Grand Saga

As the story grows, the narrative becomes persistent.

The world begins to accumulate:

- characters
- locations
- factions
- events
- history

Participants begin to feel that the world **existed before they arrived**.

Goal:

Transform a collection of stories into a **living saga**.

---

# Stage 3 — AI Safety Layer

The growing world becomes a laboratory for exploring ideas related to safe intelligence.

Through story and gameplay, participants explore concepts such as:

- cooperation
- governance
- safe evolution
- alignment
- collective decision making

Goal:

Develop a **culture of safe intelligence** before powerful AI systems appear.

---

# Stage 4 — Adventures in Gamengineering

Participants begin experimenting with game mechanics.

Examples:

- card games
- grid games
- small simulations
- experimental mechanics
- toy systems

This stage teaches participants how worlds function from an engineering perspective.

Goal:

Develop **gamengineering literacy** and create many small experimental games.

---

# Stage 5 — Game World (Game Maker)

Kaleidoscope matures into a full game creation environment.

Capabilities include:

- building games
- building game rooms
- designing interactive systems
- constructing persistent worlds

The first large game worlds begin to emerge.

Goal:

Transform Kaleidoscope into a **game maker and world builder**.

---

# Stage 6 — Creative Ecosystem

As the game worlds expand, a larger ecosystem forms.

Participants create:

- new games
- tools
- systems
- expansions
- experiments

The ecosystem becomes increasingly self-sustaining.

Goal:

Enable a **large collaborative creative ecosystem**.

---

# Stage 7 — SHAGI

The final stage explores the development of:

Supersafe Hyperadvanced Artificial General Intelligence (SHAGI).

By this stage the ecosystem already contains:

- shared culture
- governance structures
- safety philosophy
- vast testing environments

Goal:

Develop advanced intelligence systems **within a safe, cooperative environment**.

---

# Summary

The Kaleidoscope ecosystem evolves through the following stages:

1. Interactive Fiction  
2. Grand Saga  
3. AI Safety Layer  
4. Adventures in Gamengineering  
5. Game World (Game Maker)  
6. Creative Ecosystem  
7. SHAGI  

Each stage builds upon the previous one, gradually increasing complexity while preserving safety and collaboration.
