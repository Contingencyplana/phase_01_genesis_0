# Scene: scene_0101

## Title
A Sailor's Story

## Location
Harbor Village

---

## Scene Text

A weathered sailor leans against a stack of cargo crates near the docks.

His coat smells faintly of salt and old rope, and his beard is threaded with grey. He watches the harbor with the relaxed patience of someone who has spent most of his life at sea.

When he notices you nearby, he gives a small nod.

"First time on Harbor Island?" he asks.

Behind him the tide rocks the ships gently against the wooden piers.
