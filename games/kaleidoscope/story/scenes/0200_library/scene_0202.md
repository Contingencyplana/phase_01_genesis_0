# Scene: scene_0202

## Title
The Handwritten Notice

## Location
Library Hill

---

## Scene Text

You step closer to the small notice pinned beside the library door.

The paper is newer than the faded wooden sign beneath it. Someone has written the message carefully in dark ink, the letters steady but slightly uneven, as if the writer had paused often while choosing their words.

The notice reads:

**Travelers and readers are welcome.**

**This library exists to preserve the knowledge of Harbor Island and the journals of those who studied it.**

**Please handle the books with care. Some are older than the village itself.**

Below the message, a second line has been added in smaller handwriting.

**If you seek the journals of the Visionary, begin with the central table.**

There is no signature.

You glance back toward the open doorway of the library. The quiet interior waits just beyond the threshold.

From the hilltop you can hear the distant sounds of the harbor below — gulls crying, ropes creaking, merchants calling across the docks.

But here on the hill, the air feels still.

Almost expectant.
