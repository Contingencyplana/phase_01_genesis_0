# Scene: scene_1403

## Title
Where the Trail Ends

## Location
Inland Ridge Path / Final Marked Site

---

## Scene Text

The inland line leads to one more marked site, set on a high outcrop where the path opens before dropping into country no map yet shows.

The circle-and-dot mark is here.

But this time, something more.

Into the stone beside the symbol, a date has been carefully carved. The same careful hand. The same deliberate depth.

Below the date, initials: a signature.

The Visionary reached this point.

The mark shows the furthest known position of their investigation.

Here the path continues inland, but the Visionary's dated records stop.

No later notes in the station. No continuation entries. No final explanation or conclusion carved into stone. Just this mark, dated, deliberate as all the others, and final.

The line itself extends further into territory the old maps do not name. The symbol no longer leads visibly from this point; the records from Harbor Island made no note of marks beyond this position. But the inland country beyond can be reached. It can be explored. It is there.

The Visionary came this far and then... stopped leaving evidence.

Disappeared. Moved on. Succeeded, failed, or transformed into something the old records would not recognize.

The question is inherited, not answered.

The inquiry that began in Harbor Island's library continues here, at the edge of the mapped world.

What comes next remains to be discovered.
