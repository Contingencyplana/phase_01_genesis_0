# Scene: scene_1404

## Title
Close Reading at the Final Mark

## Location
Inland Ridge Path / Final Marked Site

---

## Scene Text

Up close, the date is cut with the same disciplined hand as the initials beneath it.

The strokes are careful rather than ceremonial. Nothing is embellished. Whoever carved them expected the mark to be read later, not admired.

The date places the Visionary here two seasons after the latest field entry preserved at the inland station.

That gap matters.

It means the work did not end when the notes stopped. The Visionary kept moving, kept measuring, or kept returning long enough to reach this outcrop and leave a final deliberate trace.

The initials confirm presence, but not conclusion.

They do not say why the Visionary stopped writing, what lay further inland, or whether this mark was meant as record, warning, or reassurance.

They only establish something narrower and harder:

the Visionary reached this point in person, later than the surviving papers suggest, and wanted that fact to remain.
