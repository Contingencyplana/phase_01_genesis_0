# Scene: scene_1301

## Title
The Inland Marker

## Location
Eastern Shelf / Inland Stone Face

---

## Scene Text

The eastern path rises past the split marker and narrows along a stone-cut ledge that the carrier route ignores on its way back to the civic shore.

Halfway along, set back from the path where someone would have to pause deliberately to see it, a flat stone face bears a marking cut old enough that lichen has grown back into the cuts.

A circle. A dot at its center.

Not worn smooth. Not accidental. Cut with the same deliberate depth found in the forest stone circle on Harbor Island.

Here there is no library nearby, no Visionary's notes on the table. Only the path, the shelf, and the mark.

The symbol is inland. It predates the route names on the carrier board. It is outside the coastal geography where it has always appeared before.

The archipelago does not feel larger.

It feels older.
