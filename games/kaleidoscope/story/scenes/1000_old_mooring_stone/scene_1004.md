# Scene: scene_1004

## Title
Holding Order at the Quay Margin

## Location
Edge of Tidemarket / Temporary Work Marker

---

## Scene Text

By late light, the argument resolves into a temporary order rather than a final verdict.

The rope line is shifted, not removed.

A chalk mark on the quay sets a no-move boundary around the stone until the next survey tide.

One berth plan is deferred.

A workaround lane is opened for smaller draft loads.

A clerk reads the terms aloud while a runner copies them for posting.

"Stone remains in place pending further reading. Heavy turn maneuvers rerouted. Delay claims to be filed with lane code attached."

Nobody sounds satisfied.

But work resumes in a shape people can follow.

The consequence spreads immediately:

routes need amendment,
board notices need revision,
and market sellers begin recalculating what will arrive late.

The stone has not been solved.

It has been absorbed into civic procedure, at least for now.
