## Scene Text

The path leaving the harbor village quickly narrows as it approaches the trees.

Tall pines rise along the edge of the island’s forest, their branches swaying softly in the ocean wind. The sounds of the harbor fade behind you, replaced by the quiet murmur of leaves and distant birds.

The trail continues deeper between the trees, winding into shadows where the sunlight struggles to reach the ground.

Near the beginning of the path stands an old wooden signpost. Its lettering has faded with time, but a few words remain barely visible.

Beyond the sign, the forest waits in silence.
