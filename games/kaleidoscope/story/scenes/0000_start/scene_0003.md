# Scene: scene_0003

## Title
The Library on the Hill

## Location
Library Hill

---

## Scene Text

The road from the harbor climbs steadily toward the highest point of the island.

Before long the noise of the docks fades behind you, replaced by the quiet rustle of wind through tall grass. At the top of the hill stands the old stone library.

The building is larger than you expected. Its weathered walls face the sea, and tall windows catch the pale morning light. A wooden door stands slightly ajar.

A faded sign beside the entrance reads:

**Island Library**

Below it, someone has recently pinned a small handwritten notice.

From this height the entire harbor is visible below — ships drifting slowly in the tide while the village continues its morning bustle.

The open doorway waits silently.
