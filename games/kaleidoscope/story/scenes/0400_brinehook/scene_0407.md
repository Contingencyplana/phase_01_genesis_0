# Scene: scene_0407

## Title
The Weathered Marker

## Location
Lookout Rise

---

## Scene Text

At first glance it looks like an old mooring weight set into the ground.

Up close, you can see it is carved stone, rounded by wind and grit. Most of its surface has worn smooth, but one shallow mark remains.

A circle.

Inside it, a faint dot nearly erased by time.

You hear footsteps on the path behind you. The captain's daughter pauses beside the marker and follows your gaze.

"That thing's been here longer than my father's father," she says. "Nobody around here put it up. We just stopped trying to move it."

She shrugs, practical as ever.

"Old coast keeps old things. That's all most people say."

The wind shifts, carrying the smell of tide and smoke from the cottages below.
