# Scene: scene_0405

## Title
Along the Rocky Shore

## Location
Rocky Shoreline

---

## Scene Text

You follow the edge of the island where dark stones slope into clear, cold water.

Sea birds wheel above the shallows, dropping and rising on the wind. Tide pools gather in the hollows between rocks, reflecting a pale strip of sky.

Out beyond the surf line, open water stretches between islands in long blue-gray bands.

Now and then you catch sight of a distant sail.

The shore holds no grand secret, only the patient marks of weather: smoothed stone, salt-scored wood, old rope fused stiff by sea spray.

It is enough to stand here and listen to the water working at the coast.
