## Scene Text

Beyond the wharf, a compact yard opens between a row of wind-worn cottages.

Fish racks stand on one side beneath a canvas cover. On the other, stacked crates and mended nets wait for the next run. A rust-marked anchor rests beside a bench crowded with hooks, knives, and wooden floats.

The captain's daughter steps out from a doorway carrying a basket of line weights.

"You made good time," she says to her father, then gives you a measured but friendly nod.

The captain sets down a small bundle from the boat and starts sorting gear as if this routine has played out many times before.

Around you, Brinehook moves at a family pace: work shared, tasks traded, weather watched.
