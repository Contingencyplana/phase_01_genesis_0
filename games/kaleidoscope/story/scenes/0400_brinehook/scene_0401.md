# Scene: scene_0401

## Title
Crossing the Channel

## Location
Open Water

---

## Scene Text

At first light, the old captain casts off with a quiet efficiency that comes from long habit.

You take the line he hands you, and together you ease the small sailboat out of Harbor Island's docks and into open water.

The weather is decent. A steady wind fills the sail. The channel rises and falls in broad, manageable swells.

"Keep your weight with the boat," the captain says. "No need to fight her. Just work with the water."

As Harbor Island settles behind you, another shape comes forward through the morning haze.

Brinehook Island is lower and smaller than Harbor Island. A short wharf reaches into the water beside clustered cottages and drying racks. A few working boats rock close to shore.

Nothing dramatic announces it.

People simply live there.
