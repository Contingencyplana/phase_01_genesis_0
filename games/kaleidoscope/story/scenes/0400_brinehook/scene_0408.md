# Scene: scene_0408

## Title
Return at Dusk

## Location
Brinehook Wharf

---

## Scene Text

By dusk, the wind has settled into a steady pull toward Harbor Island.

The captain checks the rigging one last time while his daughter passes him a wrapped bundle for the return trip.

"Fair water," she says. "You should make the docks before full dark."

The captain nods and casts off.

Brinehook drifts behind you: cottage lights, low roofs, and the quiet rhythm of an island closing out another day.

The crossing home feels shorter.

You have been somewhere else in the archipelago now, and come back by ordinary sail and weather.
