# Dream Thresholds

## Purpose

This file tracks the narrative thread centered on dream scenes, threshold moments, and symbolic recurrence beyond ordinary crossings.

It does not replace the registry layer.  
It exists to describe how the dream thread functions in the current Interactive Novel and how it relates to the grounded archipelago.

The dream layer is still small, but it is no longer incidental.

It is now one of the main ways the story deepens its mystery without abandoning grounded exploration.

---

## Current Role in the Story

At the current stage, dream thresholds function as a **secondary layer of pressure** beneath the grounded island world.

They do not replace ordinary travel, island life, or local story arcs.

Instead, they:

- mark meaningful thresholds
- intensify recurrence
- widen the symbolic field of the story
- suggest that some places and moments lie closer to a hidden seam than others

The dream layer is therefore not a separate world in the ordinary sense.

It is a deepening layer that presses against the grounded world.

---

## What Is Currently Established

The following elements are currently established:

- dream scenes occur after meaningful progression rather than at random
- dream scenes are tied to recurrence, symbols, and threshold pressure
- the circle-and-dot symbol appears within the dream layer
- dream figures may speak in symbolic rather than explanatory language
- dream scenes do not yet overturn grounded travel or grounded causality
- the player returns cleanly to ordinary play after the dream

This means the dream layer is already real, but still restrained.

---

## Current Dream Pattern

### Waypoint Dream

The waypoint layer establishes the idea that the player may encounter places outside ordinary waking crossings.

This introduces dream-space as something connected to progress and pattern rather than detached fantasy.

### Milestone Dream

The milestone layer deepens this by introducing stronger symbolic figures, recurrence language, and the sense that time, place, and perception may not always behave in a simple straight line.

This allows the story to name pressure without explaining it away.

---

## Relationship to the Grounded World

The dream thread must remain connected to the grounded archipelago.

That means:

- grounded islands stay primary
- dream scenes arrive as pressure, threshold, or echo
- dreams deepen the meaning of waking scenes rather than replacing them
- the player should feel continuity, not dislocation

The dream thread works best when it makes grounded scenes feel richer afterward.

---

## Relationship to Other Threads

### Visionary Mystery

The dream layer increases the pressure surrounding the Visionary by suggesting that the world contains patterns not fully accessible through ordinary scholarship alone.

### Circle-and-Dot Symbol

The symbol is one of the strongest anchors linking dream scenes to waking scenes.

Its recurrence helps the dream layer feel structurally integrated.

### Archipelago Expansion

As the grounded world expands, the dream thread gains strength through contrast.

The more ordinary, inhabited, and practical the archipelago becomes, the more meaningful dream thresholds become when they appear.

---

## What This Thread Is Doing Structurally

The dream thread currently serves several structural functions:

- it marks milestones
- it prevents the deeper mystery from becoming only archival or archaeological
- it introduces symbolic language without requiring immediate doctrine
- it gives the story a second mode of revelation
- it helps larger patterns feel felt before they are fully understood

This thread is especially important for maintaining wonder without sacrificing grounded playability.

---

## What Should Not Happen Yet

The dream layer should not yet become:

- a constant second world
- a replacement for physical travel
- a direct explanation of the whole metaphysical system
- a routine interruption after every major scene
- an excuse to abandon grounded island-building

Its value comes from restraint.

Dream thresholds should remain meaningful because they are not constant.

---

## Likely Future Growth

Possible future development may include:

- additional waypoint or milestone dreams
- stronger recurrence motifs
- dream figures who may or may not recur
- greater links between dreams and older sites
- threshold scenes that change the player’s interpretation of waking places
- eventual pressure toward a more openly mythic layer

These developments should remain paced.

The dream thread should deepen gradually rather than erupt all at once.

---

## Design Principle

Dream thresholds should remain:

- symbolic
- restrained
- cumulative
- connected to progression
- clearly related to, but not dominant over, the grounded world

A good dream scene should leave the player feeling that something deeper is pressing through the story, while still wanting to return to the islands and keep exploring.
